About

A concept built around two moods in one place.

Buenos Días Café x Por La Noche is designed as a space that changes with the day. In the morning and early afternoon, it focuses on coffee, breakfast, and a bright, welcoming café experience.

In the evening, the restaurant reopens with a different energy. The atmosphere becomes warmer and more elevated, with dinner service and a mood that feels more intimate and intentional.

This website is being built not only as a class project, but as a real concept site that could continue to grow alongside the business.
